My First Website Ever: The Biography

Background:

My names are Maduh Chibuike Justin, Frontend Engineering student of AltSchool Africa with student ID ALT/SOE/024/0951. A native of Ahizau Mbaise Imo state, Nigeria.

Education:

I had my basic education (Nur/Primary) in Owerri Imo state. I attended the glorious Goverment College Owerri for my secondary education where I performed excellently well in my West Africa Examination Council (WAEC) after which I got admitted into Federal University of Technology Owerri (FUTO). I proceeded to Kuala Lumpur Malaysia where I bagged another BSc in Business Administration, International Business major with Second Class Upper Honours. Again, I proceeded to Federal University of Technology Owerri (FUTO) for an MBA in Project Management Technology.

Family:

I came from a middle income civil servant family of 8. The third child of six children, two girls and four boys. The first of the four boys.

Work:

I started my work life as an Intern with the now defunct Bank PHB. Worked with Ray Resources Bhd Malaysia as Logistics Officer for a year before my return back to Nigeria where I started my own company Putra Global Service Ltd. Currently the Branding Manager and Partner at Esjay's Empire, A branding and Event Management Company based in Lagos Nigeria.

Hobbies:

I enjoy traveling, Driving, Football, Movies (Action&Horror),Reading.

I have always be fascinated by the internet and computers. I believed things can be done differently and better. I want to be a part of this community that will build something for the next generation.

Goals for School of Engineering:

To be a part of the success story of AltSchool Africa and a world class developer.
